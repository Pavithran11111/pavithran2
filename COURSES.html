  3. # B.COM

    4. BCom full form is Bachelor of Commerce. A BCom course is a 3-year UG degree focused on Commerce, Business, and Management. B.Com courses provide students with a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of Business, including Accounting, Finance, Economics, Marketing, and Management. The BCom course is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to pursue careers in the corporate world, entrepreneurship, or further studies in business-related fields. Students can pursue BCom courses such as full-time BCom, Distance BCom, or Online BCom.
